Ugitop


Ugitop's principal merit lies in the nature of the material: an austenitic (non-magnetic) tin and lead-free stainless steel.


It allows traditional welding and all methods of installation, in particular laying with a standing seam, and processing operations including bending, shearing, profiling, curving, etc.


Ugitop enables the design of unified building envelopes: soffits, canopies, cladding, fa√ßades (flat fascias, ribbed profiles, cassettes, etc.) and roofing.


A wide range of components and accessories provides the finishing. Its light weight, 3 or 4 kg/m2, facilitates laying on light roof frames, and due to its favourable coefficient of expansion, the strips laid reach 15 to 20 metres in a single length.
