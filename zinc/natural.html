Natural VM ZINC

When the zinc leaves the rolling mill it has a shiny, metallic surface appearance which changes over time as a natural, semi-mat, light grey patina is formed. The natural light grey zinc has a textured, luminous quality that evokes the world of minerals.

It enhances the appearance of the building from the very first day and for years to come.
